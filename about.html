About Me

Hello, the name's Maranda and I'm an avid, yet still aspiring, figure collector.

I've been building my collection slowly since the beginning of high school. For the most part, I stick with my theme of video game characters!

I've learned a good bit on how to go about buying figurines through the 5 years I've been collecting, so I thought I'd help others out.

Creation

I've often had times where I needed the helping hand of the internet to make sure I wasn't making mistakes when starting a new hobby.

I felt that I should share my knowledge with those who also need the help. The few years I've been doing this has taught me a lot after all.

You'll find Model Madness to be full of all the advice I could give to anyone trying to start their new hobby of figure collecting!

Offerings

Throughout my site, I offer helpful advice on the different types of figurines you're able to collect.

Along with this, I go over the best ways for you to manage your spending and choices before you buy.

I even offer some basics for maintaining your collection properly so everything remains looking good as new!
