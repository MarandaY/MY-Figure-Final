What are Action Figures?



Action figures are probably what you first think of when you hear the word "figurine." They're those familiar posable toys that most people had while growing up.
Although, there's significantly more variety to them than that!
Plenty of people collect action figures due to the variety you have with the added articulation. You can make your own scene with all your favorite characters to have for a fun display!

How Action Figures Differ

Many different types of action figures exist in the world. It's up to you to decide on which types you prefer. A basic rundown on a few of the different options is as follows:

  1. High-end display action figures

  2. Low-end Casual action figures

  3. Good Smile Nendoroids

The first option is fairly common in collection. These are the types of action figures that are made at a much higher quality. Their main purpose is just to be displayed after being posed. Because of their quality though, they can be quite pricey.

The second option is for if you have a tighter budget to work with, or if you just want a more casual collection. These are the types of action figures you can just buy easily from hobby stores or toy sections. They're just as valid for a collection, and they are much more affordable, they just won't have as much detail or posability.

The third option is a bit more specific, but some people like to solely collect Nendoroids for their display. They are small, chibi versions of characters from a vast amount of series. Some can be more expensive than others, but their small size typically makes them affordable while still having a very high quality to them.
