You'll find everything that you need to know here if you've been looking to get into the hobby that is starting a new collection. The Basics

Action Figures

A very popular type of figure for collecting, action figures are made to be posed however you want. Create a collection of your own dynamic poses or recreations.

Statues

Still images brought to life, statues are some of the most common figures out there. Coming in all sorts of shapes and sizes, these types of figures can make for a beautiful display without needing to pose anything yourself.

Model Kits

The DIY of the figure collecting world. Model kits are figures that you make yourself. The extra effort allows for more complex character designs with the added bonus of articulation!
